Terms and conditions


Written translations

Globus Traduzioni is at your disposal for a free quote, that will be sent out to you within the first working day after receiving your request.

In order to enable Globus Traduzioni to timely send you a customized quote please remember to indicate:
  1. Amount of words contained in the document(s) to be translated (including spaces and punctuation)
  2. Format of the document(s) to be translated (Word, PDF, TXT, etc.)
  3. Type of text to be translated
  4. Requested delivery date
  5. Delivery method of translated document(s), that is remittance by e-mail/mail or by sharing on cloud
  6. Destination address of translated document(s), should remittance by mail be requested
After receiving the request, Globus Traduzioni may contact you to obtain clarifications or additional information on the above points.
Beside the quote, the reply message by Globus Traduzioni will contain a confirmation of your requests with respect to the above-listed points. If, for any reason, it will not be possible to confirm the delivery date that you requested, Globus Traduzioni will indicate the best possible delivery date.
If the document(s) to be translated is (are) supplied in a non-editable format and/or the urgent translation of the said document(s) is requested, Globus Traduzioni reserves the right to apply a quote surcharge.
Any expenses related with the obtainment of new licensed softwares, applications, etc. that Globus Traduzioni should have to purchase in order to perform the requested service have to be entirely borne by the customer. In the interest of both parties, Globus Traduzioni undertakes to send you its free quote within the first working day after receiving your request.
If the quote by Globus Traduzioni meets your favor, approve it by e-mail and please remember to inform:
  • the e-mail or mail destination address of the translated document(s)
    • OR
  • the credentials to access the cloud server where the translated document(s) has (have) to be saved

Consecutive or liaison verbal interpretation

The rate for interpretation services is calculated by taking into account units consisting of 4 hours each.
The 4 hours every such unit consists of does not include the duration of the round trip to the service performance venue.
The minimum rate charged by Globus Traduzioni is equal to the amount due for a service demanding 1 unit (4 hours). If the service duration is either less than 1 hour or more than 10 units (40 hours), Globus Traduzioni reserves the right to apply a discounted rate.
Any food- and lodging-related expenses sustained by Globus Traduzioni during the stay at the venue where the service is delivered as well as any expenses related with the rountrip(s) to/from the said venue are entirely borne by the customer.


Job assignment cancellation

In case a previously assigned translation job is cancelled, the customer gets charged with the sum due for the portion of translation that Globus Traduzioni has already accomplished as the notification of the job assignment cancellation is received. Upon receiving the sum due, Globus Traduzioni undertakes to transfer ASAP to the customer the portion of translation already completed.

In case a previously assigned interpretation job is cancelled, out of the rate quoted by Globus Traduzioni the customer gets charged with a sum equal to:
  • 100% if the cancellation is notified on the same day the assigned job is set to begin
  • 75% if the cancellation is notified within the day before the assigned job is set to begin
  • 50% if the cancellation is notified two (2) days before the assigned job is set to begin (ex.: on day 1 for a service performance set to begin on day 3)
  • 25% if the cancellation is notified three (3) days before the assigned job is set to begin (ex.: on day 1 for a service performance set to begin on day 4)
The working day of Globus Traduzioni concludes at 7:00 p.m. (CET). In case the notification of cancellation of any job assignment is sent after the above-said time limit, Globus Traduzioni considers such notification as received on the following day.

Delayed delivery, damage, loss of translated documents

Globus Traduzioni is not liable for the delayed delivery, loss or damage of translated documents, if such inconveniences can be imputed to any interruption or anomaly in the functioning of the telecommunications network or postal service or to any other force majeure cause.
The unfortunate occurrence of one of the aforementioned inconveniences does not imply any reduction of the rate indicated in the quote previously issued by Globus Traduzioni and confirmed by the customer.
