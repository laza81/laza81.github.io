About myself

Born in Trento and residing in the multi-ethnic suburb of Gardolo, I am a native Italian speaker with remote Tyrolean maternal ancestors and a keen interest in Indo-European languages.

Beside prompting me to attend the languages high school and, later on, the university course in foreign languages & literatures, this very interest has characterized my entire professional path and - along with several inspiring travel and work experiences - it has contributed to fill my baggage with multi-sectorial knowledge and skills.

Day after day I keep walking this path accompanied by that precious baggage, inside which there is always room for new journeys, encounters, events.
